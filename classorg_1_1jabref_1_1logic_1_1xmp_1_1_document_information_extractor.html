Optional<BibEntry> org.jabref.logic.xmp.DocumentInformationExtractor.extractBibtexEntry ( )
inline

Function for retrieving a BibEntry from the PDDocumentInformation in a PDF file.

To understand how to get hold of a PDDocumentInformation have a look in the test cases for XMPUtilTest.

The BibEntry is build by mapping individual fields in the document information (like author, title, keywords) to fields in a bibtex entry.

Returns
The bibtex entry found in the document information.
