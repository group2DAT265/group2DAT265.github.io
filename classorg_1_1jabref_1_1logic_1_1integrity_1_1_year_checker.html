Optional<String> org.jabref.logic.integrity.YearChecker.checkValue ( String  value)
inline

Checks, if the number String contains a four digit year and ends with it. Official bibtex spec: Generally it should consist of four numerals, such as 1984, although the standard styles can handle any year whose last four nonpunctuation characters are numerals, such as ‘(about 1984)’. Source: http://ftp.fernuni-hagen.de/ftp-dir/pub/mirrors/www.ctan.org/biblio/bibtex/base/btxdoc.pdf
