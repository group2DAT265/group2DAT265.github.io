int compare (BibEntry e1, BibEntry e2)
 

Detailed Description

Compares Bibtex entries based on their 'crossref' fields. Entries including this field are deemed smaller than entries without this field. This serves the purpose of always placing referenced entries after referring entries in the .bib file. After this criterion comes comparisons of individual fields.
