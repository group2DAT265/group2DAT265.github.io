Detailed Description

The |built_in| function {.{purify$}} pops the top (string) literal, removes nonalphanumeric characters except for |white_space| and |sep_char| characters (these get converted to a |space|) and removes certain alphabetic characters contained in the control sequences associated with a special character, and pushes the resulting string. If the literal isn't a string, it complains and pushes the null string.
