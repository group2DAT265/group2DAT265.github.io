Detailed Description

This resize policy is almost the same as TableView#CONSTRAINED_RESIZE_POLICY We make sure that the width of all columns sums up to the total width of the table. However, in contrast to TableView#CONSTRAINED_RESIZE_POLICY we size the columns initially by their preferred width.
