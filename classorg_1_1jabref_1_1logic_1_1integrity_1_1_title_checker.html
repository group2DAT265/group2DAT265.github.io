Optional<String> org.jabref.logic.integrity.TitleChecker.checkValue ( String  value)

Algorithm:

  • remove everything that is in brackets
  • split the title into sub titles based on the delimiters (defined in the local variable DELIMITERS, currently . ! ? ; :)
  • for each sub title:
  • remove trailing whitespaces
  • ignore first letter as this can always be written in caps
  • check if at least one capital letter is in the sub title
