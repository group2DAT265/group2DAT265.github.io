From BibTex manual: One or more page numbers or range of numbers, such as 42–111 or 7,41,73–97 or 43+ (the '+' in this last example indicates pages following that don't form a simple range). To make it easier to maintain Scribe-compatible databases, the standard styles convert a single dash (as in 7-33) to the double dash used in TEX to denote number ranges (as in 7–33). biblatex: same as above but allows single dash as well
