Function for retrieving a Bib\+Entry from the P\+D\+Document\+Information in a P\+DF file.

To understand how to get hold of a P\+D\+Document\+Information have a look in the test cases for X\+M\+P\+Util\+Test.

The Bib\+Entry is build by mapping individual fields in the document information (like author, title, keywords) to fields in a bibtex entry.
