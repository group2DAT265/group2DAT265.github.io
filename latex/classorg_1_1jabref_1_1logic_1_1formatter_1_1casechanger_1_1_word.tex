\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a word in a title of a bibtex entry. 

A word can have protected chars (enclosed in \textquotesingle{}\{\textquotesingle{} \textquotesingle{}\}\textquotesingle{}) and may be a small (a, an, the, ...) word.
