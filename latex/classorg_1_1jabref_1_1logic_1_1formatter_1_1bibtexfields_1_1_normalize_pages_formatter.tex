\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This class includes sensible defaults for consistent formatting of Bib\+TeX page numbers.

From Bib\+TeX manual\+: One or more page numbers or range of numbers, such as 42--111 or 7,41,73--97 or 43+ (the \textquotesingle{}+\textquotesingle{} in this last example indicates pages following that don\textquotesingle{}t form a simple range). To make it easier to maintain Scribe-\/compatible databases, the standard styles convert a single dash (as in 7-\/33) to the double dash used in T\+EX to denote number ranges (as in 7--33).
