Get display version of a entry field. 

Bib\+TeX is case-\/insensitive therefore there is no difference between\+: howpublished, H\+O\+W\+P\+U\+B\+L\+I\+S\+H\+ED, How\+Published, etc. 

The was a long discussion about how Jab\+Ref should write the fields. See \href{https://github.com/JabRef/jabref/issues/116}{\texttt{ https\+://github.\+com/\+Jab\+Ref/jabref/issues/116}} 

The team decided to do the biblatex way and use lower case for the field names.
