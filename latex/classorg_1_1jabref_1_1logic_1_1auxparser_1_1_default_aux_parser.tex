\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
La\+TeX Aux to Bib\+TeX Parser 

Extracts a subset of Bib\+TeX entries from a Bib\+Database that are included in an A\+UX file. Also supports nested A\+UX files (latex \textbackslash{}include).

There exists no specification of the A\+UX file. Every package, class or document can write to the A\+UX file. The A\+UX file consists of La\+TeX macros and is read at the \textbackslash{}begin\{document\} and again at the \textbackslash{}end\{document\}.

Bib\+TeX citation\+: \textbackslash{}citation\{x,y,z\} Biblatex citation\+: \textbackslash{}abx@aux{\bfseries [Nested]} A\+UX files\+: @input\{x\}
