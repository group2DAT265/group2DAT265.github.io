\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This group contains entries, which were manually assigned to it. Assignments are stored in the \mbox{\hyperlink{enumorg_1_1jabref_1_1model_1_1entry_1_1field_1_1_standard_field_a5dca09f1c7a6d78739c12a7883482942}{Standard\+Field\#\+G\+R\+O\+U\+PS}} field. Thus, internally, we represent \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1jabref_1_1model_1_1groups_1_1_explicit_group}{Explicit\+Group}} as a special \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1jabref_1_1model_1_1groups_1_1_word_keyword_group}{Word\+Keyword\+Group}} operating on \mbox{\hyperlink{enumorg_1_1jabref_1_1model_1_1entry_1_1field_1_1_standard_field_a5dca09f1c7a6d78739c12a7883482942}{Standard\+Field\#\+G\+R\+O\+U\+PS}}.
