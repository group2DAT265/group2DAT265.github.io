\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Merges a list of nodes into a tree. Nodes with a common parent are added as direct children. For example, the list \{ A $>$ A1, A $>$ A2, B \} is transformed into the forest \{ A $>$ A1, A2, B\}.
