Returns the month in Jab\+Ref format. The format is the short 3-\/digit name surrounded by a \textquotesingle{}\#\textquotesingle{}. Example\+: \#jan\#, \#feb\#, etc.

See \href{https://github.com/JabRef/jabref/issues/263\#issuecomment-151246595}{\texttt{ Issue 263}} for a discussion on that thing. This seems to be an {\itshape invalid} format in terms of plain Bi\+B\+TeX, but a {\itshape valid} format in the case of Jab\+Ref. The documentation is available at the \href{https://docs.jabref.org/fields/strings}{\texttt{ Strings help}} of Jab\+Ref.
